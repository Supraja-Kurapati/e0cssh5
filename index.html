SAHARA

The Sahara is a desert on the African continent. With an area of 9,200,000sqkms (3,600,000 sq mi), it is the largest hot desert in the world and the third-largest desert overall, smaller only than the deserts of Antarctica and the northern Arctic. The name "Sahara" is derived from the Arabic word
